I fell in love with the world of technology and good design – not to mention how much it simplifies life – with the advent of smart products and services offered by companies like:

  • Google – The world’s most popular search engine that grants everyone access to the world’s knowledge.

  • Apple – A premium company that revolutionized the smartphone and wafer-thin laptop industries with its top-notch computers and smartphones (Seriously, the iPhone X is “top-notch”).

  • Tesla – Electrifying the automotive industry with its beautiful, autonomous, space-ship like vehicles that outperform super cars – even if it’s a sedan.


Having the freedom to enter into any domain of technology anywhere in the world – given a year or so of practice – and to be paid generously to do what you love:

Building things to make people’s lives simpler, safer, and that much more enjoyable to live.

Who would want to pass that up?

It is with this mindset that I hold myself accountable throughout the areas of my life.
