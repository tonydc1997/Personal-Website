About

Hey everyone! My name is Anthony Curtis and I am a developer with an interest in design living in Los Angeles.

Freedom and creativity were my entry-point into the wonderful world of technology.

Boundless Opportunities

I love the freedom and breadth of opportunities that comes with being a developer! You get to choose how you want to work (freelance, office-worker, or entrepreneur), and where (remote, office), what area you'd like to focus on (the web, software, games, a particular programming language), even the type of industry you'd like to go into. All the while collaborating with smart groups of people and having the ability to create something – beautiful, useful, efficient, life-changing – out of nothing. Who would want to pass that up?

I want to make a positive impact around me by solving complex problems, while living a life-long adventure of continuous learning and self-improvement.
